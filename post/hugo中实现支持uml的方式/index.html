### 使用mermaidjs mermaidjs

  1. 引入js文件:mermaid.min.js

    vi themes/even/layouts/partials/footer.html
<!-- mermaid JS -->
<script src="<https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/npm/mermaid/dist/mermaid.min.js>"></script>
<script>
mermaid.initialize({ startOnLoad: true });
</script>

  2. 在md文件中添加代码块

    <div class="mermaid">
